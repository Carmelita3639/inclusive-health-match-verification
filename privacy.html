Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Introduction

Inclusive Health Match ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name and contact information
  • Healthcare preferences (language, cultural background, provider preferences)
  • User reviews and ratings

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with culturally competent healthcare providers
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Communicate with you about updates and features

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. We comply with HIPAA and GDPR regulations where applicable.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with healthcare providers you choose to contact through our platform.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.
