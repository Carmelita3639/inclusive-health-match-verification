Frequently Asked Questions

What is Inclusive Health Match?

Inclusive Health Match is an AI-powered platform that connects patients with culturally competent healthcare providers based on race, ethnicity, gender, language, specialty, and LGBTQ+ affirming care needs.

How do I search for a healthcare provider?

Use the "Find Physicians" feature on the home screen. You can filter by specialty, language, gender, cultural affiliation, and other preferences.

Are the healthcare providers verified?

Yes, we verify provider credentials through license databases and professional registries. Verified providers display a "Verified" badge.

Is my information private and secure?

Absolutely. We comply with HIPAA, GDPR, and other privacy regulations. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

How much does Inclusive Health Match cost?

The basic app is free. We offer premium subscriptions at $9.99/month or $99/year for unlimited AI-powered matches and advanced features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancel anytime through your account settings or through the App Store/Google Play subscriptions management.

Can I write reviews for providers?

Yes! You can submit reviews and ratings to help other patients make informed decisions. All reviews are moderated.

I'm a healthcare provider. How do I join?

Email us at providers@inclusivehealthmatch.com to learn about our verification process.

What is the Cultural Calendar feature?

The Cultural Calendar highlights health observances, holidays, and wellness information relevant to diverse cultural backgrounds.

Does Inclusive Health Match provide medical advice?

No. We are a matching platform, not a healthcare provider. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

How do I delete my account?

Delete your account through app settings or by emailing support@inclusivehealthmatch.com.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen, or tap "Email me a login link" for secure access.

Technical Support

Quick troubleshooting tips:

  • App not loading: Check internet connection and restart the app
  • Search not working: Try clearing filters or updating to the latest version
  • Can't log in: Use "Email me a login link" or reset password
  • Profile not updating: Check internet connection and try again
  • Payment issues: Check payment method in App Store or contact your bank

Still having issues? Email support@inclusivehealthmatch.com with problem details.
